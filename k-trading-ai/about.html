A Letter from the CEO

Lessons from failure created today's DBS LABS.

Hello. This is DBS LABS.

Until now, I have created dozens of automated trading programs.

Using all kinds of indicators like RSI, MACD, and moving averages.

Despite incorporating all publicly available information, I experienced multiple liquidations.

"Why does this keep happening?"

Each time, I asked myself that question, and through analyzing my failures, I realized one important fact.

I shouldn't be obsessed with individual indicators or a single strategy.

"Liquidation is not just a single loss, but the biggest risk that destroys the balance of the entire investment strategy."

Based on this lesson, I learned that properly handling data and understanding the big picture of the market is more important than anything else.

These experiences eventually became the foundation for creating today's DBS LABS program.

Of course, you don't have to use our program.

However, if DBS LABS can serve as a benchmark to help you find the optimal method for your investment, we believe that alone is meaningful.

Our DBS LABS team members are constantly striving to provide better data-driven automated trading systems than anyone else.

Thank you.
DBS LABS Team
